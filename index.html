Campaign Performance Audit Report

Subject: Discrepancies in DBL Ad Campaign Reporting

Prepared for: Avantage Entertainment USA LLC

Date Range Reviewed: June 25 - August 15, 2025

Source Reports: DBL Performance Report & Avantage Analytics Summary

Summary of Discrepancies

Metric DBL Reported Avantage (Actual)
Total Clicks 28,334 2,359
Accounts Created 6 3
Verified Accounts 5 3
Deposits 1 0

Over 90% of DBL's claimed clicks did not result in actual tracked sessions on our platform.

Analysis of Traffic & Attribution

1. Traffic Attribution Failure:

  • DBL reports 28,334 clicks, but Avantage recorded only 2,359 users from DBL-tagged UTM links.
  • Indicates broken or missing UTM tags, redirect issues, or artificial traffic (e.g., bots).
  • This discrepancy exists across all ad creatives, not isolated to any campaign.
